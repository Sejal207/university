From Here To The World

BML Munjal University seeks to transform higher education in India by creating a world-class, innovation-led, teaching, learning and research environment. Established in 2014 by the founders of the Hero Group, BMU provides inter-disciplinary learning and transformative education that empowers students to become innovatorsand leaders who can take on the future.

Courses We Offer

At BML Munjal University, the academic structure is unique. There is a one-college concept that is embedded into the structure of the university. Rather than confining students to the silos of individual departments of commerce, engineering and management, the university has schools. With every programme offered by the university, functional disciplinary silos have been broken down and replaced with integrated and interdisciplinary, problem-solving approaches.

Industry Collaborations


BMU is creating an enabling environment for a sustainable industry-academia partnership. We seek to bridge the gap between what industry needs and the readiness of graduates when they enter the workforce. Faculty and students collaborate with enterprises to work on solutions to problems in real time. Our students gain exposure to industry-relevant knowledge and competencies, and learn life-lessons from mentors.
